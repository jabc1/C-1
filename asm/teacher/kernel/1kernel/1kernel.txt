. 内核版本和patch
	. 制作补丁:
		diff -Nur linux-2.6.30 linux-2.6.31 > 2.6.31.patch

	. 打补丁:
		cd linux-2.6.30	
		patch -p1 < ../2.6.31.patch

	. 恢复补丁:
		patch -R < ../2.6.31.patch
